INSEAD is a graduate business school with campuses in Europe (Fontainebleau, France), Asia (Singapore), and the Middle East (Abu Dhabi). "INSEAD" is originally an acronym for the French "Institut European d'Administration des Affaires" or European Institute of Business Administration. It offers a full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) programme, a Executive MBA (EMBA) programme, a Master in Finance programme, a PhD in management programme, and a variety of executive education programmes. INSEAD was founded in 1957 by venture capitalist Georges Doriot along with Claude Janssen and Olivier Giscard d'Estaing. Original seed money was provided by The Paris Chamber of Commerce.The school was originally based in the Chateau de Fontainebleau, before moving to its current Europe campus in 1967
