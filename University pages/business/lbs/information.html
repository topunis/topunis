The London Business School (LBS) is an international business school and a constituent college of the federal University of London. LBS was founded in 1964 and awards post-graduate degrees (master's degrees in management and finance, MBA, PhD and EMBA). It is widely considered to be one of the leading business schools in Europe and is one of the few schools in the world to have the triple crown accreditation LBS was ranked 1st in Europe and 2nd worldwide.
