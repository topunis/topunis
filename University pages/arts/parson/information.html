Parsons School of Design (known colloquially as Parsons) is a private art and design college located in the Greenwich Village neighborhood of Lower Manhattan in New York City. It is one of the five colleges of The New School. Parsons was the first school in the United States to offer programs in fashion design, advertising, interior design, and graphic design.Parsons offers 13 undergraduate bachelor's programs, including Illustration, Communication Design and Fashion, along with 17 graduate master's programs, and it is widely regarded as one of the most prestigious art and design schools in the world.It is currently ranked as the #1 art and design school in the United States and ranks number 2 in the world, just behind the Royal College of Art in London.
