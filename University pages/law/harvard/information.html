Harvard Law School (also known as Harvard Law or HLS) is one of the professional graduate schools of Harvard University, located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Founded in 1817, it is the oldest continually-operating law school in the United States and is generally considered to be one of the most prestigious in the world he school has a considerably larger class size than most law schools each class in the three-year J.D. program has approximately 560 students, the largest of the top 150 ranked law schools in the United States According to Harvard Law's 2015 ABA-required disclosures, 95.33% of the Class of 2014 passed the Bar exam.Harvard Law School graduates have accounted for 568 judicial clerkships in the past three years,including one-quarter of all Supreme Court clerkships,more than any other law school in the United States
